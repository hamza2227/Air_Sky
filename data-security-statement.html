We understand that making online purchases involves a great deal of trust on your part– we take this trust very seriously, and make it our highest priority to ensure the security and confidentiality of customer information. To protect your confidentiality and that of your customers, Air Sky uses leading security technology and procedures.

Internally our systems are configured with data encryption, or scrambling technologies and industry-standard firewalls. Additionally, all communications to and from Odysseus’ applications are accomplished via Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) software, which encrypts traffic both to and from your systems to ours and are facilitated by digital authentication certificates which are issued by approved and certified authorities such as VeriSign, Comodo, GoDaddy, GeoTrust, Thawte, LetsEncrypt and others. Finally, our network is continuously monitored for security events by an independent third party firm.

As a service provider, Air Sky complies with PCI DSS Level 1 requirements and hires an external QSA to perform the audit, a report of this audit is available on request for existing clients. Personal information is stored on restricted-access computers located in physically secure areas. Of course, the entire credit card number is securely transmitted to the appropriate supplier for order processing..

While we implement the above security measures, you should be aware that security is a cooperative effort between you and us, and that the measures that we have implemented can be compromised if your systems do not utilize, or misuse, the mechanisms that we have described here. Remember that after you sign in, you should sign out once you have completed your transaction, managed your account(s) or decided to step away from your computer. Anyone who accesses your computer after you have signed in but before you have signed out will have access to your account(s).

Please review your License Agreement as to certain of your rights and responsibilities for protecting the electronic information created and maintained with our products and services.

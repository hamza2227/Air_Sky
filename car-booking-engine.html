Car Booking Engine

Designed to increase revenues while it automates processes, the Air Sky Car Booking Engine reduces the amount of time and effort required to complete a Car booking. This online booking engine includes a vast amount car line, car ship and cabin descriptions linked to media files & rich content such as cabin photos, ship images and deck plans to assist in the shopping process.

Hosted by Air Sky, this Online Booking Engine’s headers, footers and style sheets are easily customized to provide a seamless online experience. Complete private label versions are also supported where Air Sky can change the complete layout to meet specific requirements. The application even uses innovative responsive technology that automatically adjusts the display layout based on the type of device used to access the application.

Live access to Car Line content is provided via Amadeus as well as direct connections with Car lines to be sure the most accurate availability and pricing is used.

Privacy Policy

Our Commitment To Privacy

Inter Travels Solutions, LLC (“Inter Travels”) knows that you care how Personally Identifying Information (“PII”) about you is used and shared, and we appreciate your trust that we will do so carefully and sensibly. This notice describes our privacy policy. By visiting a web site that communicates with or is linked to or by Inter Travels’ software, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy Notice.

The Information We Collect

Inter Travels operates solely as a provider of Internet-enabled Application Service Provider (“ASP”) that provides a comprehensive customer & travel reservation online booking & management system solution for travel sellers registered directly with Inter Travels. As such, Inter Travels does not collect any of the data within our systems except for and at the explicit instructions of our “Clients”. Our Clients are comprised of travel agencies, consortia, consolidators, online travel sellers, host agencies, suppliers and others that have contracted with Inter Travels to use our applications and services. All PII received by Inter Travels originates from our Clients, users who access our system through our Clients or users who directly visit the Inter Travels website. Our Clients have contracted with Inter Travels to act on their behalf in order to fulfill travel booking requests. Inter Travels does not collect PII from any other source. Only information required to complete a travel request (including but not limited to supplier availability search, booking) is asked for and received by our applications. Access to the Inter Travels applications & systems is done via the internet and all access is enforced over SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) also known as TLS (Transport Layer Security). SSL is widely accepted industry encryption protocol for web browsing and enforces all HTTP communication over HTTPS protocol encrypting all data including PII to be encrypted. Inter Travels does operate session-based web-enabled applications, however Inter Travels does not employ stealth PII-gathering or spyware-type devices of any kind. However, our customers may choose to employ such devices in web sites that they control that accesses Inter Travels’ applications and systems. Inter Travels does not contract with outside entities to augment or provide personal information of any kind. Inter Travels, of its own accord, does not originate, collect or augment PII in any way.

The Way We Use Information

Inter Travels does not act on any of the data within our systems except for and/or at the explicit instructions of our Clients that have contracted to use our software and services. Inter Travels passes all data that is collected through its systems to the appropriate entities in order to complete travel requests. Inter Travels does not act on, supplement or disseminate PII of its own accord in any way. Inter Travels’ applications requires that its Clients input their agent’s information, which may optionally include an employee number in addition to traditional office contact information. This information is only used to facilitate travel requests and related uses. Inter Travels’ applications and services will ask its users to provide customers’ PII in order to complete travel transactions. This will most probably include passport number and country for purposes of passing it through to the supplier to complete a travel booking. Other information collected will probably include passenger name, address, age, telephone and email contact information. Similarly, payment information is also collected for the same purposes. In cases where special, sensitive categories of PII are collected, it may optionally include voluntarily supplied customer information pertaining to ethnic, religious or lifestyle preferences-only for the purposes of the supplier providing the appropriate food and/or accommodations. Again, this information is voluntarily supplied by our Clients’ customers, through their travel agents, to Inter Travels for the purpose of passing it through to the supplier for the purposes of completing a travel requests or related uses. When data is stored on Inter Travels’s systems, it is done so only at the instruction of our Clients, again, for the purposes of enabling the travel transactions and related uses. As part of Inter Travels’s offerings of travel industry software and services, Inter Travels’s applications will send email confirmations to customers at points in travel transactions. Inter Travels does not email PII as part of this feature. Similarly, Inter Travels’s systems may occasionally be the source of email promotional campaigns. However, Inter Travels will only do so at our Clients request, and only with data that belongs to that Client. Again, Inter Travels [will not knowing?] be the source of that which is emailed, but the ultimate control of this rests with the discretion of our Client. Inter Travels does provide its Clients the ability to provide their customers with opt-out capabilities for promotional mailings, but the ultimate implementation of this feature is at the discretion of our Clients. Inter Travels will only use PII that is provided for the benefit of our Clients, which includes, but is not limited to responding to travel and travel-related requests, customizing future travel reservations and communicating with Clients and their customers.

Our Commitment To Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. Please refer to Inter Travels Solutions Terms & Conditions for further details.

Our Commitment To Children’s Privacy

Protecting the privacy of the very young is especially important. Inter Travels does not provide or operate software or web sites that in any way is intended to attract anyone under the age of 13. Similarly, Inter Travels will never disseminate PII about children of its own accord.

How You Can Access Or Correct Your Information

Inter Travels does not originate or augment PII in any way, and strongly suggests that individuals contact the travel agency or website responsible for originating the PII for corrections and disputes. In the event that a PII issue cannot be resolved with the originating party, Inter Travels can be contacted directly. Inter Travels reserves the right to charge a service fee to help cover the cost of researching and coordinating the remediation of any PII issue.

Notification of Changes

Due to the rapidly evolving technologies on the Internet, we may occasionally revise or update this policy. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on our corporate homepage so our users are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under circumstances, if any, we disclose it.

How To Contact Us
